Shopping Places

Places to Shop

Fernweh

Fernweh is a small boutique on main street of Midway. This shop includes tuff like candles, souvenirs, hygiene care products, and clothing. Clothing ranges from jackets, to Midway engraved merch, and winter gear.

St. Lawrence Thrift Store

St. Lawrence Thrift Store has a variety of used and new items all ranging from clothes, shoes, kitchenwear, and home decor. They have stuff for very affordable prices if you're on a budget.

Famous Footwear

Famous footwear is a shoe store providing a variety of shoes and sandals. They have well-known brand names like croc, birkenstock, and converse.
