Fun Things To Do

Things To Do

Ziplining

Ziplining during the summer is a fun activity. Located near Deer Creek State Park, this ziplining adventure provides a view of the Deer Creek lake and mountains surrounding it.

The Zermatt Hotel

The Zermatt hotel in Midway offers lots of fun acitivites to do with family. There's a huge swimming pool to make use of during the summer and there's also a miniature golf course to enjoy some time in the ourdoors with the gorgeous mountain views!

Wasatch Mountain State Park

Wasatch Mountain State Park is a beautiful activity and a must on your bucket list. You enjoy the peace of the outdoors and while at it, you're getting those steps in hiking through the park. It's a more memorable experience if you go during the fall time!
