About Heber

Heber is known for it's beautiful landscape and mountains. Founded back in 1859 by Robert Broadhead, James Davis, and James Gurr, Heber is at an elevation of 5595 feet. Although Heber is small, there's so much to do. We have a bowling alley, great food places, beautiful hiking places, and much more!
